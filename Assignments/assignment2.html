LIGHTING AN LED

The LED has two leads of different lenghts, the longer one is the anode and the shorter one is the cathode. Current flows from the anode to the cathode therefore to light an LED you must connect the anode tho the higher potential and the cathode to the lowest potential. if the voltage is bigger the LED is brither, if the voltage is smaller the LED is less bright
