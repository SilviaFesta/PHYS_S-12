Assigment 1: Designing components with fusion 360

STEPS:

  1. Choose the components to design

  2. Measure with a caliber

  3. Create sketches

  4. Extrude sketches

  5. Assembly

1 CHOOSE COMPONENTS TO DESIGN:

I have chosen to start by designing something simple as component 1 (infact this component can be designed by extruding one single sketch) and than try something more complex as a screw.
